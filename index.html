Camilla DePaola

Camilla DePaola celebrated 106 years of a joyous life. Her loving spirit has been inspirational to all who have been fortunate to know her. She was grateful for every moment that she could be in the company of others. All were welcome to share in her laughter, her wit, her wisdom. Through her humanity Camilla taught us that life is about loving and caring for each other. She will be sadly missed.
